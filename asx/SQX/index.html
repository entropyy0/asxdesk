FAQs

What does SQX do?

Sqx Resources Limited is an Australian mineral exploration company primarily focused on exploring for critical minerals and base metals across its tenement portfolio located in prospective regions of Western Australia. The company aims to identify and define economic mineral resources.

Is SQX a good investment?

Investing in SQX is highly speculative, typical for an early-stage mineral explorer with a small market cap. While successful exploration could lead to significant returns, there's a substantial risk of exploration failure, funding challenges, and commodity price volatility. Potential investors should be aware of the high-risk, high-reward nature.

SQX's share price is primarily driven by exploration news, including drilling results, geophysical survey outcomes, and any updates on resource definition.
