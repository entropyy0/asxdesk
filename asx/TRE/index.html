Toubani Resources Limited (TRE)

Toubani Resources Limited (TRE) is an ASX-listed minerals exploration company focused on discovering and developing base and precious metal projects, primarily in West Africa. Leveraging local expertise, TRE aims to unlock value through targeted drilling and resource definition. Key properties include the Kati-Zedna copper-zinc project in Mali and the Gossi gold project in Mali.
