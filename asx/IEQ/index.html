FAQs

What does IEQ do?

IEQ is a real estate company based in Perth, Western Australia. Its primary business is the ownership and operation of the Seasons of Perth hotel, which generates the vast majority of its revenue.

Is IEQ a good investment?

IEQ is a high-risk, speculative investment suitable only for investors with a high tolerance for risk. Its appeal lies in the potential disconnect between its low market cap and the value of its underlying property, but this is balanced by significant risks including single-asset dependency, high debt, and very low stock liquidity.

What drives IEQ's share price?

IEQ's share price is primarily driven by the profitability of its hotel operations, changes in the assessed valuation of its Perth property, and any corporate activity such as a potential sale of the asset or a takeover offer. The broader health of the Western Australian economy and tourism market is a key external factor.
