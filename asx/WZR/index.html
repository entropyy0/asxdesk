FAQs

What does WZR do?

Wizr Limited (ASX: WZR) is an Australian financial technology company that provides consumer personal loans through a fully digital platform. It aims to offer 'better debt' solutions to customers, leveraging technology for efficient loan origination and management.

Is WZR a good investment?

Investing in WZR is speculative due to its small market capitalization and position as a growth-oriented fintech lender. While it offers potential upside from a growing loan book and market share in consumer finance, it also carries risks related to credit quality, funding costs, competition, and achieving sustainable profitability. Investors should consider their risk tolerance.

What drives WZR's share price?

WZR's share price is primarily driven by its loan book growth and quality, its ability to achieve and maintain profitability, net interest margins, and funding costs. Broader economic conditions affecting consumer lending, regulatory changes, and competitive landscape within the Australian fintech sector also significantly influence investor sentiment.
