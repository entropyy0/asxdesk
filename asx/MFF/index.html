FAQs

What does MFF do?

MFF Capital Investments Limited (MFF) is an Australian-listed investment company (LIC) that invests in a concentrated portfolio of high-quality global listed equities, aiming for long-term capital growth and income for its shareholders.

Is MFF a good investment?

Investing in MFF offers exposure to a professionally managed, concentrated portfolio of global equities, potentially providing diversification and long-term capital growth. However, it carries risks associated with global market volatility, currency fluctuations, and the possibility of its shares trading at a discount to its Net Tangible Assets (NTA).

What drives MFF's share price?

MFF's share price is primarily driven by its Net Tangible Assets (NTA) per share, which reflects the value of its underlying global investment portfolio. Other factors include investor sentiment towards global equity markets, the company's dividend policy, and the perceived performance and track record of its fund manager.
