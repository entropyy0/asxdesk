Reckon Limited (RKN)

Reckon Limited (RKN) is an Australian information technology company specializing in software solutions for accounting, financial management, and practice management. Primarily operating in Australia and the UK, key products include Reckon Practice Management, Reckon Accounting, andFLOW. The company serves accounting firms, businesses, and individuals.
