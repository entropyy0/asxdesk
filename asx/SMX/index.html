What does SMX do?

Is SMX a good investment?

As an early-stage micro-cap exploration company, SMX is a highly speculative investment. It offers significant upside potential if exploration success is achieved but carries substantial risk, including potential for capital loss, as its value is tied to discovery and commodity prices.

What drives SMX's share price?

SMX's share price is primarily driven by news of exploration success, particularly high-grade drilling results and resource definition updates.
