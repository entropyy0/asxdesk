Roolife Group Ltd (RLG)

Roolife Group Ltd is a cross-border e-commerce company focused on connecting Australian and international brands with Chinese consumers. The company operates an online B2B2C marketplace, selling health, wellness, and food & beverage products through major Chinese platforms like Tmall and JD.com. RLG also provides associated digital marketing, social media, and logistics services to help brands enter and grow in the Chinese market.
