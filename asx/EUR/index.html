European Lithium Limited (EUR)

European Lithium Limited (ASX: EUR) is an ASX-listed lithium miner focused on developing the Huhnergrube lithium deposit in Austria. The company aims to become a mid-tier lithium producer supplying the European EV and renewable energy markets with high-grade lithium carbonate. Operations are primarily centered in Austria with corporate functions in Australia.
