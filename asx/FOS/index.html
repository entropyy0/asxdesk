Fos Capital Ltd (FOS)

Fos Capital Ltd (ASX: FOS) is an Industrials sector player focused on capital goods, operating primarily in Australia. Specifically, FOS invests in and supports early to growth-stage companies within the industrial technology and capital goods space, aiming to drive innovation and scalability. Its key services include strategic investment, operational support, and network facilitation to its portfolio companies.
