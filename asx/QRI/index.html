Qualitas Real Estate Income Fund (QRI)

Qualitas Real Estate Income Fund (QRI) is an ASX-listed investment entity focused on providing direct exposure to a diversified portfolio of real estate debt investments in Australia, targeting regular income. Since its listing in November 2018, QRI has operated through Qualitas, a real estate specialist with over two decades of experience. Its key product is the provision of mezzanine finance and other debt solutions to property developers.
