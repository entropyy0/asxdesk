Centuria Capital Group (CNI)

Centuria Capital Group (CNI) is an Australian real estate company operating as an Equity Real Estate Investment Trust (REIT), focused on diversified investment portfolios across commercial, retail, and industrial sectors primarily within Australia. Key services include property management, development, and advisory. The company operates nationwide with a strong presence in major metropolitan areas.

What does CNI do?

Is CNI a good investment?

CNI offers a relatively stable income stream through its dividend yield, appealing to income-seeking investors. However, its growth potential and valuation are sensitive to the broader Australian property market trends and interest rate changes, making it important for investors to weigh these factors.

What drives CNI's share price?
