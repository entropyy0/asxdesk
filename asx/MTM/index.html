Metallium Limited (MTM)

Metallium Limited (ASX: MTM) is an exploration and development company focusing on nickel, cobalt, and lithium projects in Australia, primarily in Western Australia's nickel-rich regions. Its key projects include the Carradale Nickel-Cobalt Project, highlighting its strategic focus on battery metals. Listed on the ASX in July 2021, MTM aims to capitalize on the growing demand for critical minerals.
