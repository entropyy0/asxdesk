FAQs

What does REA do?

REA Group operates digital property portals, primarily realestate.com.au in Australia, providing online advertising for residential, commercial, and rural properties. It also offers property data, financial services (e.g., mortgages), and invests in international property platforms.

Is REA a good investment?

REA Group is a market leader with a highly profitable business model and strong growth potential driven by digital migration and international expansion. However, it faces risks from housing market cyclicality, macroeconomic conditions, and potential competition, requiring investors to consider both its dominant position and sensitivity to external factors.

What drives REA's share price?

REA's share price is primarily driven by the health of the Australian housing market (listing volumes, property values), interest rate expectations, and the uptake of its premium 'depth' products by agents. Performance of its international ventures and strategic investments, alongside broader macroeconomic trends, also play a significant role.
