Ora Banda Mining Ltd (OBM)

Ora Banda Mining Ltd (ASX: OBM) is an Australian gold exploration, development, and production company focused on its flagship Ora Banda Gold Project in the Western Australian goldfields. The company operates the Davyhurst processing plant and multiple open-pit and underground mines, aiming to be a significant mid-tier gold producer by optimising existing assets and expanding its resource base.

FAQs

What does OBM do?

Ora Banda Mining Ltd is an Australian gold producer engaged in the exploration, development, and mining of gold deposits within its Ora Banda Gold Project in Western Australia, including operating the Davyhurst processing plant and multiple open-pit and underground mines.

Is OBM a good investment?

Investing in OBM offers exposure to gold price movements and potential upside from exploration success and production growth in a tier-one mining jurisdiction. However, it carries inherent risks associated with commodity price volatility, operational execution, and resource depletion, requiring careful consideration of its financials and future plans within the volatile mining sector.

What drives OBM's share price?

OBM's share price is primarily driven by the prevailing spot price of gold, the company's quarterly gold production and cost performance (All-In Sustaining Costs - AISC), significant exploration drilling results, and updates to its mineral resource and ore reserve estimates.
