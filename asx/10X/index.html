Exultant Mining Limited (10X)

Exultant Mining Limited is a junior mineral exploration company focused on the discovery of critical minerals in Western Australia. The company's primary activities involve exploring its portfolio of highly prospective lithium and rare earth element (REE) tenements. As an early-stage explorer, its operations are centered on geological surveying and drilling programs to define potential resources.
