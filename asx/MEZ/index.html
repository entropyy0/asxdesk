Meridian Energy Limited (MEZ)

Meridian Energy Limited (ASX: MEZ) is a large-cap New Zealand-Australian utilities company listed on the ASX, primarily generating and supplying renewable energy, predominantly from hydroelectricity. Operating across both New Zealand and Australia, key products include electricity sales to residential, commercial, and industrial customers. MEZ is known for its commitment to 100% renewable energy generation.
