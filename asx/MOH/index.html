FAQs

What does MOH do?

Moho Resources Limited is an Australian mineral exploration company that explores for nickel, platinum group elements (PGEs), gold, and rare earth elements across its tenements in Western Australia.

Is MOH a good investment?

Investing in MOH is highly speculative due to its micro-cap status and early-stage exploration focus. While there's potential for substantial returns if a significant discovery is made, there's also a high risk of capital loss if exploration is unsuccessful or funding becomes challenging. It's suitable only for investors with a high-risk tolerance who understand the exploration mining sector.

What drives MOH's share price?

MOH's share price is primarily driven by exploration news, particularly drilling results, resource updates, and new project acquisitions. Broader commodity price movements for nickel, gold, and PGEs also play a significant role, as do successful capital raises and overall market sentiment towards junior explorers on the ASX.
