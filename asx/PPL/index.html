Pureprofile Ltd (PPL)

Pureprofile Ltd is a global data and insights organisation providing online research and digital advertising services. Operating across Asia-Pacific, Europe, and North America, the company connects brands with consumers through its proprietary research panels and programmatic media platform, Pure.amplify. Its core services include audience building, data collection, and insights generation for market research and targeted advertising campaigns.

FAQs

What does PPL do?

Pureprofile is a data and insights company. It operates a platform that allows businesses to conduct market research by surveying consumers from its large online panels, and also offers a programmatic advertising service (Pure.amplify) that uses this data for targeted digital marketing campaigns.

Is PPL a good investment?

As a small-cap tech company in a turnaround phase, PPL is considered a speculative investment. Potential upside exists if it can continue to grow its revenue and achieve consistent profitability, leveraging its valuable first-party data. However, risks include intense competition, sensitivity to economic conditions, and the inherent volatility of small-cap stocks.

What drives PPL's share price?

PPL's share price is primarily driven by its financial performance, especially revenue growth and its ability to achieve and maintain positive EBITDA and cash flow. Key catalysts include the announcement of major new client contracts, successful geographic expansion, and positive quarterly Appendix 4C reports demonstrating operational progress.
