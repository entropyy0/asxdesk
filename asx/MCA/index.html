Murray Cod Australia Limited (MCA)

Murray Cod Australia Limited (ASX: MCA) is an Australian aquaculture company focused on the sustainable, land-based production and sale of premium Murray Cod under its 'Aquna Sustainable Murray Cod' brand. Utilising innovative recirculation aquaculture systems (RAS) in regional NSW, the company supplies high-end restaurants, hotels, and increasingly, export markets with a premium, clean-tasting fish product.
