FAQs

What does VAR do?

Variscan Mines Limited (ASX: VAR) is an Australian mineral exploration company focused on exploring for and delineating zinc-lead-silver resources. Its primary operational base is in Northern Spain, where it holds several prospective projects, most notably Novales-Udias.

Is VAR a good investment?

Investing in VAR is highly speculative, typical for a micro-cap mineral exploration company. While there's significant upside potential if exploration yields a major discovery, there's also substantial risk of capital loss if drilling results are disappointing or funding becomes scarce. It's suited for investors with a high-risk tolerance and a long-term outlook.

What drives VAR's share price?

VAR's share price is primarily driven by the outcomes of its exploration activities, particularly drilling results and resource updates. Other key factors include the prevailing prices for zinc, lead, and silver, success in capital raising, permitting progress in Spain, and general market sentiment towards junior mining explorers.
