Felix Group Holdings Ltd (FLX)

Felix Group Holdings Ltd (ASX: FLX) provides a cloud-based enterprise procurement software platform specifically designed for the construction and infrastructure industries. Its Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform helps large project owners and operators manage their supply chains, streamline contractor procurement, and reduce risk. The company primarily operates within Australia and New Zealand.

What does FLX do?

Is FLX a good investment?

As a small-cap ASX-listed technology company, FLX represents a speculative investment opportunity.
