Lgi Limited (LGI)

LGI Limited (ASX:LGI) is an Australian utilities company focused on renewable energy solutions, operating across Eastern Australia. Key products/services include solar panel installations, wind turbine maintenance, and grid-connected energy storage systems. Established in 2022, LGI targets residential and commercial markets.
