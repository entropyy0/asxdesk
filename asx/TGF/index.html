Tribeca Global Natural Resources Limited (TGF)

Tribeca Global Natural Resources Limited (ASX: TGF), listed in 2018, is an investment firm focusing on global natural resource assets, providing capital to emerging and growth-stage mining and energy companies, primarily in Australia and internationally. Key products/services include equity investments, debt financing, and strategic partnerships in the resources sector.

Is TGF a good investment?
