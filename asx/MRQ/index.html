FAQs

What does MRQ do?

MRQ Metals Limited is an Australian-listed mineral exploration company focused on identifying and developing significant graphite resources, primarily through its Carrego and Corridor Graphite Projects located in Mozambique.

Is MRQ a good investment?

Investing in MRQ is highly speculative and carries significant risk, typical of micro-cap exploration companies. While it offers potential upside from successful graphite exploration and development in a growing market, it is balanced by the inherent uncertainties of mineral discovery, funding requirements, and commodity price volatility.

What drives MRQ's share price?

MRQ's share price is primarily driven by exploration success (drilling results, resource upgrades), progress towards project development (feasibility studies), and investor sentiment towards the graphite and critical minerals sectors. Capital raising activities and general market conditions for junior explorers also play a significant role.
