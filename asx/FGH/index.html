Foresta Group Holdings Limited (FGH)

Foresta Group Holdings Limited (ASX: FGH) is an Australian biomaterials company focused on producing high-value, sustainable biographite from renewable pine plantations. The company is developing a proprietary technology to convert wood biomass into a range of graphite and carbon products, primarily targeting the burgeoning electric vehicle (EV) battery market and other industrial applications. It aims to establish production facilities initially in Australia with global market ambitions.
