Osteopore Limited (OSX)

Osteopore Limited (ASX: OSX) is a medtech company developing proprietary 3D printed scaffolds for tissue regeneration, focusing on bone repair. Operating in Australia with products under development for global markets, its key product is a bioresorbable scaffold for orthopedic and craniofacial applications. Based in Perth, Western Australia, it leverages local research partnerships.
