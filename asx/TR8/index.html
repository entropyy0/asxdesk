FAQs

What does TR8 do?

Tarrina Resources (TR8) is an ASX shell company transitioning from mineral exploration to the medical cannabis industry via the acquisition of Huno Pty Ltd. Its future operations will focus on the cultivation, production, and distribution of medicinal cannabis products in Australia, pending a re-listing on the ASX.

Is TR8 a good investment?

TR8 is a highly speculative investment. Any potential upside is tied to the successful re-listing of the company and the effective execution of its new medical cannabis strategy in a competitive market. It faces significant risks, including regulatory hurdles, operational challenges, and the potential failure to re-comply with ASX listing rules.

What drives TR8's share price?

As the company is suspended, there is no active price. Upon an anticipated re-listing, key drivers will be the successful completion of the Huno acquisition, progress on obtaining TGA licenses, updates on facility construction, and achieving first revenue from product sales.
