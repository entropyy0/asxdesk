FAQs

What does DCC do?

DigitalX Limited (DCC) provides access to the digital asset market primarily through its DigitalX Bitcoin Fund, which allows sophisticated investors exposure to Bitcoin. They also offer blockchain consulting and advisory services to help businesses navigate and implement blockchain technology.

Is DCC a good investment?

As a micro-cap company operating in the highly volatile digital asset sector, DCC is considered a speculative investment. It offers exposure to the growth of the crypto market but comes with significant risks related to price fluctuations, regulatory changes, and intense competition. Potential investors should weigh the opportunities against the inherent risks.

What drives DCC's share price?

DCC's share price is primarily driven by the performance of the broader cryptocurrency market, especially the price of Bitcoin, given its Bitcoin Fund. Other key drivers include growth in its Funds Under Management (FUM), success in securing new blockchain consulting contracts, regulatory developments in Australia and globally regarding digital assets, and the company's financial results.
