FAQs

What does AUB do?

Aub Group operates as an equity-based network of general insurance brokers and underwriting agencies primarily across Australia and New Zealand, and with a growing presence in the UK. They hold significant equity stakes in their partner businesses, providing them with strategic support, technology, and access to insurer markets, while benefiting from the collective network's premium generation.

Is AUB a good investment?

AUB offers exposure to the resilient insurance sector with a proven growth-by-acquisition model, potentially providing stable recurring revenue and expansion opportunities. However, as a A$3.6B mid-cap company, it carries risks related to successful integration of acquisitions, regulatory changes, and economic sensitivity impacting insurance premiums. Investors should consider its balance sheet strength and dividend policy alongside its growth strategy.

What drives AUB's share price?

Aub Group's share price is primarily driven by its acquisition pipeline and integration success, growth in underlying gross written premiums within its network, and the general insurance cycle (impacting premium rates). Financial performance metrics like earnings per share growth, dividend declarations, and broader market sentiment towards the financial services sector also play a significant role.
