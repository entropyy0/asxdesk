Reach Resources Limited (RR1)

Reach Resources is a Western Australian-focused critical minerals explorer primarily targeting rare earth elements (REEs) and lithium. The company's flagship projects, including Morrissey Hill and Wabli Creek, are located in the highly prospective Gascoyne mineral province. Reach is actively advancing its exploration programs through drilling and sampling to define a significant resource to supply the growing clean energy and technology sectors.

FAQs

What does RR1 do?

Reach Resources (RR1) is a junior mineral exploration company focused on discovering critical minerals, particularly rare earth elements (REEs) and lithium, at its projects in the Gascoyne region of Western Australia.

Is RR1 a good investment?

Investing in RR1 is highly speculative. The potential for significant returns exists if the company makes a major economic discovery, but there is also a high risk of capital loss due to the inherent uncertainties of mineral exploration and its reliance on external funding.

What drives RR1's share price?

RR1's share price is primarily driven by exploration news such as drilling and assay results, announcements of new discoveries, sentiment in the critical minerals sector, successful capital raisings, and corporate activity in its region.
