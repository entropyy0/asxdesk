Brambles Limited (BXB)

Brambles Limited (ASX: BXB) is a global leader in supply chain logistics, primarily known for its CHEP brand of reusable pallets, crates, and containers. Operating on a circular economy model, the company serves customers across consumer goods, fresh produce, beverage, and retail sectors worldwide. Its extensive network and pooling system aim to enhance supply chain efficiency and sustainability for its diverse client base.
