Matrix Composites & Engineering Limited (MCE)

Matrix Composites & Engineering Limited (MCE) designs, develops, and manufactures advanced polymer matrix composites used in harsh environment applications, primarily servicing the global oil and gas industry. Operating from facilities in Western Australia, MCE's key products include high-performance composite vessels, pipes, and pressure control systems. The company also explores opportunities in renewable energy and other industrial sectors.
