Norwood Systems Limited (NOR)

Norwood Systems is a telecommunications technology company that develops and provides virtual mobile services and applications. Their key products, including World Voicemail and the Corona platform, are designed to reduce international roaming costs and enhance voice communication quality for businesses and individuals. The company's technology integrates with existing mobile networks to deliver these services globally.

FAQs

What does NOR do?

Norwood Systems is a telecommunications software company that provides virtual mobile services. Its core products aim to reduce international roaming costs, improve voice call quality, and offer advanced voicemail services through apps that integrate with a user's existing mobile number.

Is NOR a good investment?

NOR is a high-risk, speculative investment typical of a micro-cap technology stock. Potential upside is tied to the successful commercialisation of its platforms and securing large-scale contracts, while significant risks include ongoing cash burn, the need for future funding, and intense competition.

What drives NOR's share price?

The share price is primarily driven by news flow such as new contract wins with enterprise or telco clients, quarterly cash flow reports (Appendix 4C) indicating revenue growth or reduced cash burn, and the success of any capital raisings.
