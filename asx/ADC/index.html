Acdc Metals Ltd (ADC)

ACDC Metals Ltd (ASX: ADC) is an Australian critical minerals exploration company focused on developing a portfolio of rare earth elements (REE), copper, and gold projects. The company's primary assets are located in Victoria, Australia, including the Heywood and Goschen Central (REE) projects and the Mighty Murray Project (copper/gold). Listed recently, ADC aims to identify and delineate economically viable mineral deposits in key resource regions.

What does ADC do?
