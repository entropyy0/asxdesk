Corazon Mining Limited (CZN)

Corazon Mining Limited is an Australian mineral exploration company primarily focused on advancing its wholly-owned Lynn Lake Nickel-Copper-Cobalt Project in Manitoba, Canada, a former nickel mining centre. The company also holds gold and copper-cobalt exploration interests in Colombia and New South Wales, Australia, respectively, seeking to discover and define battery metal and gold deposits.

What does CZN do?

Is CZN a good investment?

Investing in CZN is speculative and carries high risk due to its early-stage exploration status and small market capitalization.
