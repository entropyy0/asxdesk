FAQs

What does BBT do?

Betr Entertainment (ASX: BBT) is an Australian-based online bookmaker. It offers fixed-odds betting on a wide range of horse racing, harness racing, greyhounds, and sporting events through its mobile app and website.

Is BBT a good investment?

BBT is considered a high-risk, high-reward speculative investment. Its potential lies in its ability to disrupt the concentrated Australian wagering market and grow rapidly. However, it faces intense competition, regulatory risks, and is currently unprofitable, relying on its cash reserves to fund growth.

What drives BBT's share price?

The share price is primarily driven by its reported growth in customer numbers and betting turnover, its cash burn rate, and its progress towards achieving profitability. Market sentiment towards the tech and wagering sectors, as well as any regulatory news, also significantly impacts its valuation.
