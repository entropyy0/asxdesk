Strike Resources Limited (SRK)

Strike Resources Limited (ASX: SRK) is an Australian exploration and development company focusing on mineral resources, particularly iron ore and lithium. The company operates primarily in Western Australia and Peru. SRK's key project is the San Jorge Iron Ore Project in Peru. With a market capitalization of A$12 million, SRK is considered a micro-cap, speculative investment.
