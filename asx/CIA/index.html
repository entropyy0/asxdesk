Champion Iron Limited (CIA)

Champion Iron Limited (ASX: CIA) is a Canadian-based iron ore producer focused on developing and operating high-grade iron ore projects in the Labrador Trough region of Quebec. The company's primary asset, the Bloom Lake Mine, produces a premium quality direct reduction grade iron ore concentrate, positioning it advantageously in global steelmaking markets due to its low impurity profile.
