  • • Continued strong demand for used vehicles, benefiting from cost-conscious consumers and ongoing new car supply constraints in the market.

FAQs

What does TRA do?

TRA operates as New Zealand's largest integrated automotive group, specializing in used vehicle sales, providing automotive finance and insurance services, and managing damaged vehicle auctions.

Is TRA a good investment?

TRA offers an integrated business model with diversified revenue streams and a strong market position in New Zealand. However, as a small-cap company in the cyclical consumer discretionary sector, it carries inherent risks from economic downturns and competition, making it a speculative investment with both growth opportunities and potential volatility.

What drives TRA's share price?

Key drivers for TRA's share price include overall consumer confidence and discretionary spending, trends in the used car market, interest rate movements affecting its finance arm, and its operational performance across its sales, finance, and insurance segments.
