Pro Medicus Limited (PME)

Pro Medicus Limited (PME) is an Australian health care technology company focused on diagnostic imaging solutions. Headquartered in Melbourne, it operates predominantly in Australia and internationally, offering products like Xercise, MedMovie, and iMIX, which cater to medical imaging, patient engagement, and imaging data management. Key services include imaging software and related support.
