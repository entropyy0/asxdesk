Cedar Woods Properties Limited (CWP)

Cedar Woods Properties Limited (ASX: CWP) is an Australian property development and management company operating predominantly in metropolitan areas of Australia, with a focus on residential, retail, and mixed-use developments. Key products include land lots, apartments, and townhouses primarily in Western Australia, Victoria, and Queensland. The company also manages commercial and retail properties.
