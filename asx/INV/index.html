FAQs

What does INV do?

Investsmart Group Limited (ASX: INV) is an Australian financial services provider that offers online investment advice, managed funds, and tools for wealth management. They primarily target retail investors seeking accessible and tailored investment solutions via digital platforms.

Is INV a good investment?

As a micro-cap company with an A$15M market cap, INV is a speculative investment. It presents potential upside from growth in its FUM, client base, and successful digital strategy, particularly in a favorable market. However, investors should be aware of significant risks including market volatility, intense competition, regulatory changes, and its smaller scale, making it more suitable for investors with a higher risk tolerance and a long-term view.

What drives INV's share price?

INV's share price is primarily driven by its Funds Under Management (FUM) growth, profitability and earnings performance, and overall investor sentiment towards the Australian financial services and fintech sectors. Key factors also include successful client acquisition and retention, technological innovation in its platform, and the broader economic and regulatory environment impacting financial advice and investment markets.
