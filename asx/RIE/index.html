Riedel Resources Limited (RIE)

Riedel Resources Limited is a mineral exploration company primarily focused on its flagship Kingman Gold Project in Arizona, USA. The company is exploring for high-grade, shallow gold and silver mineralization in a historically productive mining district. RIE also holds the Marymia Project in Western Australia, which is prospective for gold and base metals.

FAQs

What does RIE do?

Riedel Resources is a junior mineral exploration company searching for gold and silver. Its main focus is the Kingman Gold Project in Arizona, USA, where it is actively drilling to define a resource.

Is RIE a good investment?

RIE is a high-risk, speculative investment suitable for investors with a high tolerance for risk. Success hinges on exploration discovery, which could yield significant returns, but failure could result in a substantial loss of capital.

What drives RIE's share price?

RIE's share price is primarily driven by its drilling and assay results from the Kingman Project. Other major factors include movements in the price of gold and silver, general market sentiment for junior explorers, and news of capital raisings.
