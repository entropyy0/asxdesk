Gwa Group Limited. (GWA)

Gwa Group Limited (ASX: GWA) is an Australian manufacturer of bathroom hardware and plumbing products operating across the Asia-Pacific region. Their key products include taps, sinks, toilets, and bathroom accessories under brands like Britex, VitrA, and plumex. They supply to both residential and commercial construction markets.
