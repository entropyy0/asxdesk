Spenda Limited (SPX)

Spenda Limited (ASX: SPX) is an Australia-based provider of intelligent transaction processing and web-based e-commerce solutions. Operating across the APAC region, the company specializes in embedded payments technology for Point of Sale (POS) and e-commerce platforms. Key products include its omnichannel payment gateway and POS integration services for the retail and hospitality sectors.
