Charter Hall Long Wale Reit (CLW)

Charter Hall Long Wale Reit (ASX: CLW) is an A$2.7 billion mid-cap Australian real estate investment trust focused on acquiring and managing high-quality, long-wale properties. Its portfolio typically comprises essential service assets leased to government, healthcare, education, and social infrastructure tenants, providing highly stable and predictable income streams through long-term leases.
